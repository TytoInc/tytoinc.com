What is Tyto Inc?

Tyto, Inc. is developing mobile applications that allow small to medium scale operations a better view of their logistics while assessing efficiency and value. Current applications are designed for multiple machine agricultural or small trucking supply type systems employing semi-repetitive path operations. Additional applications will help assess efficiency and value of the system, business or land.
