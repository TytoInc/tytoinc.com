What is Tyto Inc?

Tyto Inc focuses on mobile solutions that allow operations a better view of their logistics while assessing efficiency and value.

Applications

Agriculture:

Mobile solutions for communicating real time information across various types of equipment or personnel to increase efficiency and value to the operation.

Local Trucking and Hauling Operation

Mobile solutions providing updated arrival time, cycle time, and location information for semi repetitive loop operations.

Benefits

Agriculture

Agricultural operations which include semi repetitive operations with changing locations or field operations where there may also be a need to monitor distribution/delivery of product (planting, spraying, post harveset grain hauling) or collection/harvest of grain/products and transportation to storage.

Trucking/Hauling

Small to mid size trucking operations with semi repetitive supply routes but changing loading and/or unloading sites. Examples might include hauling gravel, lumber harvest, road paving operations.
